# Analytics and Dashboards in Power BI Project

**Overview:**  
This project focuses on data visualization and analysis using Power BI. The goal was to transform raw sales and demographics data into interactive dashboards that provide actionable insights. The project demonstrates the ability to create visuals, measures, and dashboards that answer specific business questions.

**Tools Used:**  
- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX Measures and Calculations  
- Matrix, Scatter, and Map Visualizations  
- Conditional Formatting, Slicers, and Cards  
- Microsoft Word (for written analysis)

**Skills Demonstrated:**  
- Data modeling and preparation for visualization  
- Creating interactive dashboards with multiple types of visualizations  
- Applying DAX calculations for business insights  
- Analyzing trends over time and across demographic segments  
- Professional presentation of results for decision-making

**Project Highlights:**  
- Tab Q1: Matrix of total purchases by company and year, highlighting highest totals.  
- Tab Q2–Q3: Visualizations of total purchases over time and by credit card type.  
- Tab Q4: Analysis of items viewed per session by race and gender.  
- Tab Q5: Geographical visualization of items by city, identifying trends and outliers.  
- Tab Q6: Scatter plot showing relationship between items viewed and purchased.  
- Tab Q7: Percent of items purchased out of items viewed with measure creation and customer insights.  
- Tab Q9: Sum of purchases by city with slicers for credit card type.  
- Tab Q10: Custom dashboard with multiple visualizations and interactive elements, showcasing deep analytical insights.

**Files Included:**  
- `Project3_LastName.pbix` — Power BI dashboard file  
- `Project3_LastName.docx` — Written analysis and explanations for each tab

**Outcome:**  
The project demonstrates the ability to convert raw datasets into professional, interactive dashboards that answer business questions, highlight trends, and support data-driven decisions.
